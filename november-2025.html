IEEE R10 HTC 2025

Kiyoharu Aizawa, Takao Terano
General Co-Chairs, IEEE R10 HTC2025 Local Organizing Committee

IEEE R10 Humanitarian Technology Conference (HTC) 2025 was held from 29 September to 1 October 2025, at Chiba University of Commerce in Ichikawa City, Chiba Prefecture, Japan. The theme of this year’s conference was,
